What exactly do I do?

I help startups and growing companies design better tech products, whether that means building an MVP, fixing UX issues, or refining an existing product to scale. I also bring your digital concepts to life by creating visually appealing interfaces by designing the concepts, programming languages, and industry expertise to create custom and functional UI solutions.

What exactly do I get with the MVP?

• Product Strategy Workshop (1-day session)
• Live, clickable prototype (Minimum Lovable Product)
• User testing setup
• A roadmap for future iterations

What does My UX Sprint include?

Day 1: UX Audit → I analyze user flows & friction points
Day 2: Rapid Redesign → I fix usability issues
Day 3: Testing & Handoff → You get final designs + a Loom walkthrough

Do I offer ongoing support?

Yes! I will offer retainer-based design support for teams that need continuous improvements at no extra costs.

Can I redesign your SaaS/web app?

Yes! I can audit and redesign individual screens or optimize key user flows without disrupting current operations.

How long does the process take?

Rapid MVP Build → A few days to 2-3 weeks
UX Sprint → 3 days (per user flow)
Custom Projects → 4-8 weeks+ (depending on scope complexity)

How do I pick the right service?

If you need a working prototype fast → MVP Build
If you have a product with UX issues → UX Sprint
If you need a redesign or feature launch → Custom Project
If you need ongoing support → Retainers

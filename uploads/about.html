OUR MISSION

PUTTING BUSINESSES
OF EVERY SIZE
ONLINE & AHEAD

South Africa has hundreds of thousands of businesses — from spaza shops and salons to growing SMEs and large enterprises — run by talented people who deserve technology that actually works for them.

LulaSync builds the digital tools that help those businesses grow: websites, AI agents, custom software, automation, and enterprise systems — all priced in ZAR, built in South Africa.

HOW WE WORK

01

Understand Your Business

We start with a conversation — via WhatsApp, email, or a call. You tell us what you need, who your customers are, and what problems you want solved. No forms, no jargon.

02

Scope & Quote

We propose the right solution for your budget and timeline — a website, an AI agent, a custom app, or a full enterprise system. You get a clear quote in ZAR before any work starts.

03

Build & Deliver

We build, test, and deliver your product — fast. Simple sites go live in days. Complex systems are delivered in phases so you see progress early and can give feedback throughout.

04

Support & Grow

We don't disappear after launch. Monthly support plans keep your product running, updated, and improving — and our agent network helps more businesses find us every day.

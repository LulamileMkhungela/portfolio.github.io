// Story

Who I am

I'm Lulamile Mkhungela — a product-minded designer and builder based in South Africa. I specialise in turning messy business problems into clear digital products: native and web apps, SaaS dashboards, design systems, conversational UX, and WhatsApp-first tools for real operators.

Across 7+ years I've led work from discovery through hi-fi design, design-ops pipelines, and engineering handoff — including enterprise environments (e.g. Vodacom internal products), inclusive financial UX, niche 0→1 products, and multi-platform builds.

I founded LulaSync to put that craft to work for South African businesses of every size — from kasi operators to SMEs and large enterprises — with ZAR pricing and products that actually ship.

I work solo by default. I own strategy, UX/UI, product decisions, and delivery end-to-end. When a project needs extra hands (specialist engineering, testing, content, or volume), trusted individuals step in for that scope only — then I remain the single point of accountability you deal with.

// Operating model

Solo — until specialists are needed

Day to day: me

Discovery, product framing, UX/UI, design systems, frontend-ready handoff, client communication, and shipping decisions — one person, clear ownership.

When others step in

Only if the work truly needs it — e.g. heavy engineering, QA automation, or parallel production. They contribute to a defined slice; they don’t replace my lead role.

What you get

No agency telephone game. You brief me once, get senior craft, and a single throat to choke — with optional specialists only when the scope justifies it.

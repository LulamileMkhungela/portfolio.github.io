// South African context

Design for how people
actually live.

LulaSync is grounded in the realities of South African users and businesses: mobile-first access, WhatsApp as a daily channel, uneven connectivity, local languages, trust, affordability and the need for products that work on ordinary devices. I bring that context into every decision without making the experience feel limited or generic.

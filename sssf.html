// What is the SSSF?

Free money.
Not a loan.

🏛️
Government funded

The SSSF is a South African government programme designed to help small and micro businesses grow by funding technology, equipment, and operational costs.

🎁
Grant, not a loan

This is free money. You do not pay it back. There is no interest. There is no monthly repayment. It is yours to use for your business.

📋
For small businesses

Designed specifically for micro and small enterprises — including informal businesses. You do not need to be a registered company to qualify.

💻
Technology eligible

Building a business app qualifies as an approved use of SSSF funding. LulaSync builds your app using the grant.

// Eligibility

Do you qualify?
Most do.

The eligibility requirements are straightforward. Check the list below.

✓ South African citizen or permanent resident
✓ Registered or informal business (both OK)
✓ Been operating for 6+ months
✓ Annual turnover under R10 million
✓ Not already receiving similar government funding
Not 100% sure? Complete the eligibility check below — our team will confirm within one business day.

// Common Questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Is this a loan? +
No. It is a grant — free money from the government. You do not pay it back. Ever.
How long does it take? +
Typically 4–8 weeks from submitting a complete application to receiving approval. LulaSync keeps your application moving.
What if I am not approved? +
LulaSync can still build your app from R1,500 upfront or from R300/month on a Rent track — no grant needed. You never have to walk away empty-handed.
Does LulaSync charge for applying? +
No upfront cost. We only earn a 3% referral fee if your grant is approved and the funds are disbursed to you.
Can I apply if my business is informal? +
Yes. Informal businesses — including market traders, street vendors, and home-based operations — are eligible. You do not need a registered company.
What documents do I need? +
Typically: a valid South African ID, proof of business address, 3 months of bank statements, and a brief description of your business. LulaSync will guide you through exactly what is needed for your specific application.
Can the grant fund an app I already have? +
The SSSF funds technology development and operational upgrades. If you need to upgrade, rebuild, or expand an existing app, this can qualify. LulaSync will confirm this during your free eligibility assessment.

Still have questions? Message us on WhatsApp and we will answer within a few hours.

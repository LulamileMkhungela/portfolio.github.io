How I
show up

Simple rules I work by, with clients and with others.

(01)
Do good work

If I do something, I do it well. I focus on work I'll definitely excel at, prioritise quality over quantity, and make decisions grounded in data, instinct, and common sense. I finish strong—always.

(02)
Be good people and have fun

I take my work seriously, but not myself. I work with people whom enjoy collaborating with, communicate clearly, and treat everyone with respect—no exceptions.

(03)
Own it

I have strong opinions and stand by them—always in the best interest of the work. Passion is a must, complacency is a deal-breaker, and success is shared.
Your wins are our wins

(04)
Better than yesterday

I learn, adapt, and push myself forward every day. Growth beats experience, speed beats perfection, and standing still isn’t an option. Evolve or dissolve.

(05)
Impact aware

I measure what matters—success isn’t a guess. I work against clear metrics and KPIs for ourselves and our projects, making sure every decision drives real impact. No fluff, no vanity metrics—just results that move the needle.
